User Experience Focused

Our application promotes trust in the buyer-seller relationship. No more buying "lemons" and junkers! With CarChain, you can see exactly what a vehicle has been through and how much it should be worth by comparing vehicles with similar histories. Artificial intelligence systems work to compare prices and show trends in vehicle makes and models.

Comprehensive Data Analytics

The immutable and comprehensive history of a vehicle provides value to more than just they buyer! Insurance companies would find these records useful to give a more accurate and personalized statement to their customers. Manufacturers would also find this resource useful to track the lifecycle of their vehicles.

Why Use Blockchain Technology?

The blockchain technology allows our ledgers to be trustful and immutable. This means that the information on a car is 100% accurate, since it is inputted by trusted sources and peer-reviewed.
